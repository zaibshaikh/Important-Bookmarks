A Collection of Links

For Front End Developers & Designers

Hello! Thanks for checking this out.

You may be asking yourself what this website is for. Well, one day I realized my bookmarks folder was overflowing, disorganized, and hard to navigate. So, I decided to make what I had always hoped my bookmarks folder to look like.

This site is essentially a visualization of my bookmarks folder, and also a resource for other developers and designers. I am by no means an expert on any of the topics covered by these links, but I do know a thing or two about front end development and design. Some of the links below even helped me decide that I want a career as a front end developer. I hope you find this site resourceful, useful, or anything in between.
